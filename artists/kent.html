Harold (Harry) Wan

Untitled

2D, 3D, MIXED MEDIA · 2026

As an artist and an aspiring architect, my work is driven by experimentation and exploration. From the beginning of my time in AS Studio Art, I have been drawn to working across multiple mediums, techniques, and styles to inform my understanding of the world and deepen my passion for architecture. Through close observation and an awareness of perspective, my work reflects a balance between structural thinking and creative expression.


Over the course of AS Studio Art, my process has evolved from tentative exploration into a more confident, intentional approach. What began as a fascination with materials, textures, and patterns has grown into a practice of iteration. Inspired by maps and patterns, my work is often continuous, each iteration informing the next. This process is often visible in my animations and large-scale sculptures, where experimentation and iteration play a crucial role in the finished work.


My work pushes me to balance discovery and control. While my interest in architecture pushes me towards structure and clarity, my artistic practice gives me room for exploration and experimentation. Through this balance, I continue to challenge myself, using my projects as an opportunity to explore the unknown.
