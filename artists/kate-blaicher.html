Kate Blaicher

Animal Farm

2D, 3D, MIXED MEDIA, FASHION DESIGN · 2026

I am an artist who cares deeply about animals and the environment. My fashion drawings represent animals and the importance that they carry in our environment. My architecture models represent the ability to reuse materials and to work cohesively with the environment. My large drawing represents the ability to reuse, along with showing the ocean environment. The piece with the recycled cans represents the beauty of making new with old, and how beautiful animals are. I think it's important for people to realize the value of our environment and the animals that are in it, and that not everything needs to be made new.
